Multiple Backup Strategies
Choose the right backup approach for your needs. Our platform supports:
  • Full Backups - Complete point-in-time snapshots of your data
  • Differential Backups - Only changes since the last full backup
  • Incremental (Restic) - Efficient chunk-level backups with deduplication
All backups are encrypted using AES-256 before leaving your servers, ensuring complete data security.
